Penguins are flightless birds perfectly adapted to the cold. They are skilled swimmers, using their wings like flippers. Emperor penguins are the tallest and heaviest of all penguins.

  • They can dive deeper than 500m to catch fish.
  • Penguins huddle together for warmth during Antarctic winters.
  • Their black-and-white body provides camouflage while swimming.
